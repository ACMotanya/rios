Mr. Rios has professionally distinguished himself through his eager pursuit and advocacy of licensure, involvement in local organizations such as the American Institute of Architects, and development of a diverse portfolio in the healthcare, commercial, and residential sectors. As a Tampa native and Jesuit grad (’06), he is deeply invested in the development of the local community. Much of who he has become stems from the work ethic and values instilled in him by his family. Outside of architecture, Mr. Rios is actively pursuing his fitness goal of dunking a basketball (quite a challenge for someone who’s 5’-9 ¾”) and can often be found exercising along the downtown Tampa Riverwalk. Some of his favorite local spots include Alifia State Park, Weedon Island Preserve, Sail Pavilion, and Ybor.
