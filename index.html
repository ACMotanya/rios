Alex Rios, AIA, NCARB, CDT, LEED AP BD+C

President

Much of who Mr. Rios has become stems form the values and work ethic instilled in him by his family. He demonstrates an eager desire to achieve success on every project and maintains a disciplined approach to project constraints and deadlines. As a Tampa native and Jesuit grad ('06), he is deeply invested in the development of the local community.

Outside of architecture, Mr. Rios is actively pursuing his fitness goal of dunking a basketball, and can often be found exercising along the downtown Tampa Riverwalk. Some of his favorite local spots include Alifia State Park, Weedon Island Preserve, Sail Pavilion, and Ybor City.
